Winning My Allstar Game

2014

In 7th grade it was my senior year of ABC League football. Every year the league allows teams to pick two seniors to send them to an all star game.
After my or last regular season game my coach came to me and told me that I would be the only all star from our team that year. At first I was disappointed that I was the only East Brady kid, but after the first few practices I made some amazing friends.
